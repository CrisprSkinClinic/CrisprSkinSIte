---
question: "How is dandruff different from a dry scalp?"
tags: ["dandruff"]
---

Dandruff is usually driven by Malassezia yeast overgrowth or an underlying condition like seborrheic dermatitis, often with oilier, larger flakes -- while a dry scalp produces smaller, drier flakes from lack of moisture. They're treated differently, which is why an evaluation helps before choosing a product.
