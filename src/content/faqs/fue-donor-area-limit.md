---
question: "Is there a limit to how many grafts can be taken from the donor area?"
tags: ["hair-transplant-fue"]
---

Yes. The donor area has a finite, genetically determined supply of permanent hair, and overharvesting it to chase a higher graft count can permanently reduce density there and limit options if a second session is ever needed later. Graft planning is done conservatively around your actual donor supply rather than a target number.
