Skin ageing involves several distinct, simultaneous processes -- loss of collagen and volume, changes in texture and pigmentation, deepening of expression lines, and gradual loss of skin firmness -- and no single product or procedure addresses all of them. An effective anti-ageing plan usually combines a solid skincare foundation with targeted procedures matched to whichever specific changes are actually present and bothering you.

We approach this as a genuinely individual assessment rather than a package deal, since two people who both say "I want to look less tired" may need completely different treatment -- one might benefit most from addressing volume loss, another from texture and pigmentation, another from softening expression lines.

## What to Expect at Your Visit

We assess your skin comprehensively across the different dimensions of ageing -- texture, pigmentation, expression lines, volume, and firmness -- and talk through your history with skincare, sun exposure, and any previous treatments. Just as importantly, we ask what actually bothers you and matters to prioritize, since not every visible change needs to be treated, and some patients want to address one specific thing rather than everything at once.

## Treatment Approach

A well-structured skincare routine using evidence-backed active ingredients is the foundation most other treatment builds on, and we spend real time getting this right since it's often more consequential long-term than any single procedure. Beyond that, specific procedures -- chemical peels for texture and pigmentation, Botox for expression lines, fillers for volume -- are selected based on your particular pattern of ageing, not bundled as a default package.

Sun protection is treated as a genuinely core part of any anti-ageing plan, not a footnote, since ongoing UV exposure actively works against whatever else is being done and is the single most significant modifiable driver of premature skin ageing.

## Setting Realistic Expectations

Anti-ageing treatment, across every approach available today, manages and slows visible signs of ageing -- it does not stop or reverse the ageing process itself. We build plans around realistic, meaningful improvement in the specific concerns that matter to you, communicated honestly rather than promised as a complete transformation.
