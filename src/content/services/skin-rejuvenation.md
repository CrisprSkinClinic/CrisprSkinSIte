Skin rejuvenation is a broad term for treatment aimed at improving overall skin quality -- texture, tone, radiance, and hydration -- rather than addressing one specific condition or sign of ageing in isolation. It's often what people are looking for when they describe wanting their skin to simply "look healthier" without a more specific complaint in mind.

Because "improve overall skin quality" can mean different things depending on what's actually driving the dullness or unevenness someone's noticing -- sun damage, dehydration, textural irregularity, or a combination -- we start with a proper assessment rather than defaulting to a fixed package of treatments.

## What to Expect at Your Visit

We assess your skin comprehensively, including texture, tone, hydration, and general radiance, and review your current skincare routine, since gaps or mismatches there are often a meaningful part of the picture. We'll ask what specifically prompted you to seek this out, since that context helps prioritize where to focus.

## Treatment Approach

Chemical peels are commonly used to improve texture, tone, and radiance as part of a rejuvenation plan, often combined with a properly tailored skincare regimen that extends and supports results between in-clinic sessions. The specific combination and sequencing of treatments is planned around your particular skin, not applied as an identical package regardless of individual concerns.

## Setting Realistic Expectations

Skin rejuvenation produces genuine, visible improvement in overall skin quality for most patients, but it's a gradual process built on a course of treatment plus consistent skincare -- not a single procedure with an instant, dramatic transformation. We discuss a realistic timeline at your consultation so progress is measured against an accurate expectation from the start.
